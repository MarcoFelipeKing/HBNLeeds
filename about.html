About Healthy Buildings Network Leeds

Building bridges across disciplines, councils, and industries.

Our Story & Mission

Healthy Buildings Network Leeds emerged from a cross-disciplinary vision at the University of Leeds to foster meaningful collaboration on how buildings impact health, well-being, and sustainability. We actively engage across campuses, councils, industries, and communities—locally and globally.

Through seminars, workshops, funded projects, and dynamic partnerships, HBN drives innovative research and actionable insights for healthier indoor environments.

Meet the Founders

Martín López-García

Co-Founder & Director

Martín is an associate professor in the School of Mathematics and focuses on urban sustainability, indoor air quality, and environmental health, bridging academic research and practical applications.

Irene Mussio

Co-Founder & Director

Irene is a lecturer in the Leeds University Business School and specialises in interdisciplinary collaboration, environmental risk assessment, and the translation of research into policy and practice.

Marco-Felipe King

Co-Founder & Director

Marco-Felipe is a lecturer in the School of Civil Engineering and whose expertise includes modeling environmental exposure and transmission risks, with a strong commitment to inclusive, evidence-based policy influence.
