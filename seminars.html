Past Seminars

Missed a seminar? View highlights from our recent sessions below. Where available, recordings and presentation slides are linked for on-demand access.

Liora, Chuck & Pete Apps (Grenfell)

Date: January 15, 2025

In this session, our distinguished guests discussed lessons learned from the Grenfell tragedy, the importance of building safety regulations, and the role of community engagement in preventing catastrophic outcomes. Attendees gained insight into emerging fire-safe technologies and best practices for occupant communication.

Dr. Liora’s Healthy Air Quality Workshop

Date: December 10, 2024

Dr. Liora led a dynamic workshop focusing on indoor air quality monitoring methods. Participants learned how to measure and interpret pollutant data, implement cost-effective ventilation strategies, and engage with policy frameworks that govern building health standards.

Chuck’s Innovative Building Sensors Presentation

Date: November 5, 2024

Chuck shared the latest developments in sensor technology for energy efficiency, occupant well-being, and data-driven facility management. The interactive demo allowed attendees to experiment with real-time sensor feeds, highlighting how IoT systems can revolutionize healthy building practices.
