Healthy Buildings Network Leeds

Hyper-local to global

Welcome to the Healthy Buildings Network Leeds, where we collaborate on research, innovation, and education for healthier, more sustainable buildings and their environments. Born out of a Horizons Challenge Network at the University of Leeds, we empower collaborations across disciplines and between academia, industry, and the public.

Join Us

What We Do

We connect experts, practitioners, and community members to explore how buildings impact health and well-being. Our activities include seminars, funded research projects, and collaborative initiatives to make indoor environments better for everyone.

What Are Healthy Buildings?

Mental & Physical Health

Healthy buildings enhance physical and mental well-being by fostering supportive social, economic, emotional, and environmental indoor climates.

Sustainability

Buildings that minimize environmental impact while maximizing occupant comfort through efficient resource use and smart design.

Innovation

Embracing new technologies and approaches to create spaces that actively contribute to occupant health and environmental stewardship.
